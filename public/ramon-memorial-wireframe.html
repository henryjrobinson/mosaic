Talk with Ramon

Using recordings from his lectures and videos, we've created a way to have a conversation with Ramon. Ask him about life, teaching, or just hear one of his famous dad jokes.

How do I know if I'm making the right career choice?
Well, let's think about it this way... A career is like a garden. You plant seeds not knowing exactly how they'll grow, but with patience and care, something beautiful emerges. The 'right' choice is the one where you can grow while helping others flourish too.
